Requirements

  • A wireless-network-ready computer
  • The corporate "guest" network name
  • The guest pass (a text "key")

Connecting

Finding the Wireless "Guest" Network

1 Check the wireless icon on your device.
2 Open the wireless network list.
Select your guest network.
3 Click Connect.
4 If prompted, click Connect Anyway.
5 Wait until status shows Connected.

Logging into the Network as a Guest

1 Open a browser and go to any website.
2 Accept the portal security warning if shown.
3 Enter your Guest Pass and click Sign In.
4 Disable popup blocker if needed.
5 You can now browse the internet.
6 Click Sign Out when finished.

Important

Guest access allows internet browsing only. Services such as file sharing, corporate email, or printers are not available.
